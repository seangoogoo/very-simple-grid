Very Simple Grid system for DIVI

This grid system is conceptually based on the one of Bootstrap 5.2 in an extremely simplified way and by adapting it to the three thresholds of DIVI: 479px, 768px and 980px.

The default behavior of a 'div.s-row' is to get any of its direct childs to behave as a 'div.s-col-12', in other words to get 100% witdh of its parent.
